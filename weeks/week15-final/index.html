Unit 9 Grammar Review

Time Contrasts & if Conditionals

Time Contrasts (시제 대조)

시간 표현에 따라 올바른 시제를 선택합니다:

TimeMarkersTense
🔙 PastA few years ago, In the pastSimple Past
📍 PresentThese days, Nowadays, TodayPresent Simple/Continuous
🔜 FutureSoon, In the futurewill / be going to / might

First Conditional (1차 조건문)

If + present, will/may/might + base V

If you study hard, you 'll pass the exam.

⚠️ Common Mistake

✗ If you will study hard, you'll pass.

✓ If you study hard, you'll pass.

if절에는 will 사용 금지! 현재형을 씁니다.

Unit 10 Grammar Review

Gerunds & Short Responses

Gerunds after Verbs (동명사)

다음 동사/구 뒤에는 -ing형을 씁니다:

Verbs + -ingPhrases + -ing
love, enjoy, hate, don't mind, can't standbe good at, be interested in, be tired of
I enjoy helping people.

Short Responses: So do I / Neither do I

긍정 동의부정 동의
"I love cooking." → "So do I.""I don't like sports." → "Neither do I."
"I'm creative." → "So am I.""I can't swim." → "Neither can I."

⚠️ Common Mistake

✗ I enjoy to help people.

✓ I enjoy helping people.

enjoy, love, hate 뒤에는 -ing형! (to부정사 X)

Unit 11 Grammar Review

Passive Voice

Passive Past with by (과거 수동태)

S + was/were + p.p. + by + agent

Disneyland was opened by Walt Disney in 1955.

Passive Present without by (현재 수동태)

S + is/are + p.p. — 행위자가 불분명하거나 중요하지 않을 때

Rice is grown in many Asian countries.

⚠️ Common Mistake

✗ The pyramids built thousands of years ago.

✓ The pyramids were built thousands of years ago.

수동태에는 반드시 be동사(was/were) + 과거분사!

Unit 12 Grammar Review

Past Continuous & Present Perfect Continuous

Past Continuous vs. Simple Past

was/were + -ing (배경 동작) + when + simple past (끼어든 동작)

Background (길게 진행 중)Interruption (짧게 끼어듦)
I was having lunchwhen the phone rang.
While I was driving,I got a flat tire.

Present Perfect Continuous

have/has been + V-ing — 과거에 시작해 지금도 계속되는 동작

I 've been studying English for three years.
for + 기간 (for 3 years), since + 시작점 (since 2023)

⚠️ Common Mistake

✗ I have been knowing him for 5 years.

✓ I have known him for 5 years.

상태 동사(know, like, be)는 진행형 불가!

Unit 13 Grammar Review

Participles as Adjectives & Relative Pronouns

Participles as Adjectives (-ing vs. -ed)

-ing (원인/사물)-ed (경험/사람)
The movie is boring.I am bored.
The news is surprising.We are surprised.
The trip was exciting.The students were excited.
-ing = 감정을 일으키는 것 / -ed = 감정을 느끼는 사람

Relative Pronouns (관계대명사)

For PeopleFor ThingsBoth
whowhichthat
A teacher who inspires...A book which became...The actor that won...

⚠️ Common Mistake

✗ I was very boring during the lecture.

✓ I was very bored during the lecture.

사람이 느끼는 감정은 -ed! (boring = 지루하게 하는)

Unit 14 Grammar Review

Modals for Speculation & Permission/Obligation

Modals for Speculation (추측)

확신도에 따라 다른 modal을 사용합니다:

확신도ModalExample
🟢 높음 (90%)mustHe must be tired. (틀림없이 ~일 것이다)
🟡 중간 (50%)may / couldShe may be busy. (아마 ~일 것이다)
🔴 낮음 (30%)mightIt might rain. (혹시 ~일지도)

Permission / Obligation / Prohibition

허가 ✅의무 ⚠️금지 ❌
can / be allowed tohave to / have got tocan't / not allowed to
You can park here.You have to wear a seatbelt.You can't smoke here.

⚠️ Common Mistake

✗ He can be tired. (추측의 의미로)

✓ He must / might be tired.

추측에는 can 사용 불가! must/may/might/could를 씁니다.

Unit 15 Grammar Review

Unreal Conditionals & Past Modals

Unreal Conditionals (가정법 과거)

현재 사실과 반대되는 상상:

If + simple past, would/could/might + base V

If I had more money, I would travel around the world.
실제로 돈이 없으므로 여행할 수 없다는 뜻! (비현실적 조건)

Past Modals: should have / shouldn't have

과거에 대한 후회나 조언:

PatternMeaningExample
should have + p.p.~했어야 했다You should have studied harder.
shouldn't have + p.p.~하지 말았어야 했다I shouldn't have stayed up late.

⚠️ Common Mistake

✗ If I would have more time, I would exercise.

✓ If I had more time, I would exercise.

if절에는 would 사용 금지! 과거형(had)을 씁니다.

Unit 16 Grammar Review

Reported Speech: Requests & Statements

Reported Requests (간접 명령/요청)

told/asked + someone + to + base V

DirectReported
"Arrive on time."She told me to arrive on time.
"Don't be late."She told me not to be late.

Reported Statements (간접 서술)

said (that) / told someone (that) + 시제 후퇴

DirectReported (시제 후퇴)
"I am sick."He said he was sick.
"I have a headache."She said she had a headache.
"I will call you."He said he would call me.
"I can help."She said she could help.

⚠️ Common Mistake

✗ She told me to don't be late.

✓ She told me not to be late.

부정 간접 명령: not to + base V (to don't X)

⚠️ Key Mistakes to Avoid

한국인이 자주 하는 실수 TOP 8

1. If you will study hard, ... → study (if절에 will X)

2. I enjoy to cook. → cooking (enjoy + -ing)

3. The pyramids built by Egyptians. → were built (be + p.p.)

4. I have been knowing him. → have known (상태동사 진행형 X)

5. The movie was bored. → boring (사물 = -ing)

6. He can be tired. → must/might (추측에 can X)

7. If I would have money, ... → had (if절에 would X)

8. She told me to don't go. → not to go
