Unit 4 Grammar Review: Simple Past vs. Present Perfect

단순 과거 vs. 현재완료 & 순서 부사

TenseUseExample
Simple Pastcompleted action at a specific time (특정 시점의 완료된 행동)I ate lunch at noon.
Present Perfectexperience or action connected to now (경험 또는 현재와 연결된 행동)I have eaten sushi before.
Present Perfectwith ever/never/already/yetHave you ever tried kimchi?
순서 부사: First, Next, Then, After that, Finally — 절차나 과정을 설명할 때 사용합니다.

1. I _____ never _____ Thai food before.

2. She _____ to Japan last summer.
